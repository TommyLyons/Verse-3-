# **App Name**: Verse3 Records

## Core Features:

- Studio Booking: Allow users to book the recording studio by selecting dates and times. Manage availability and scheduling.
- Merchandise Store: Enable the sale of merchandise, records, EPs, and vinyls directly through the website. Payment processing integration included.
- Music Upload: Admin interface for uploading and publishing music tracks from Verse3 Records artists, including DJ Lofty. Music section will feature the latest releases.
- Artist Profiles: Dedicated pages for featured artists with biographies, discographies, and links to their music.
- News and Events: Blog or news section to announce upcoming events, new releases, and other updates from Verse3 Records.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Yellow (#FFD700), taken from the logo, to represent energy and creativity. This will be used for highlights and accents.
- Background color: Black (#121212) to create a sleek and modern feel that makes the content stand out.
- Accent color: White (#FFFFFF) for text and essential UI elements, providing contrast and readability against the dark background.
- Font pairing: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for headings to maintain a contemporary, geometric style, and 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text to improve readability.  This pairing keeps a consistent aesthetic, matching sans-serif with sans-serif.
- Use sharp and modern icons. All icons should be in yellow to ensure visibility against the dark backdrop.
- Implement a grid-based layout to maintain order and responsiveness across different screen sizes. Utilize negative space to draw attention to content.
- Subtle animations for transitions and interactions, such as fade-in effects on content load, and hover effects on interactive elements. Should reinforce the brand without being distracting.
